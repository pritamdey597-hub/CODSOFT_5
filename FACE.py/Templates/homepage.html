Face Detection & Recognition

Realtime face detection (webcam), face detection in videos, and photo detection — all powered by MediaPipe and OpenCV, presented via Streamlit.

Realtime detection: Access your webcam and detect faces live.
Video detection: Upload a video and get an annotated output with face boxes.
Photo detection: Upload a photo and see face bounding boxes and keypoints.
Open Streamlit App Source (optional)
Tip: Allow camera access when asked. If running remotely, use a browser that supports webcam streaming for Streamlit.
